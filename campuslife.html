The Campus Life and Infrastructure

The library

The Institute has an excellent Library containing over 8000 books, 60 National and International journals and periodicals, variety of magazines and News papers.

The Canteen

The College Canteen continues to be meeting arena for all kinds of discussions, while enjoying nutritional food/snacks in a clean and hygienic environment, at reasonable rates.

Work Shop & Laboratory

The workshop is equipped with state of the art machines to provide practical engineering training. Machine Shop, Fitting Shop, Welding Shop Carpentry Including Pattern Shop Smithy Shop Foundry Shop

Bus Facility

The Institute is situated on National highway between Indore and Mhow and is easily accessible from city with intercity bus-service. The Institute has many personal buses , which cover every nook and corner of Indore.



Sports

Student teams of boys and girls also participate in inter college, university and other tournaments in football, kho-kho, badminton, volley ball, table tennis, carom, chess and other games.
